## 3. Kluczowe wytyczne EROD / WP29 aktywne w HR

- **WP29 Opinion 2/2017 on data processing at work (wp249)** — https://ec.europa.eu/newsroom/article29/item-detail.cfm?item_id=610169 — 9 scenariuszy (rekrutacja, in-employment screening, monitoring ICT, czas pracy, wideo, pojazdy, udostępnianie stron trzecim, transfery); endorsed przez EROD
- **Guidelines 3/2019 video devices** — https://www.edpb.europa.eu/our-work-tools/our-documents/guidelines/guidelines-32019-processing-personal-data-through-video_en — monitoring wideo w pracy
- **Guidelines 05/2020 on consent** (v1.1) — "unlikely to be freely given" od pracownika — https://www.edpb.europa.eu/sites/default/files/files/file1/edpb_guidelines_202005_consent_en.pdf
- **Opinion 28/2024 on AI models (GDPR)** — 17.12.2024 — https://www.edpb.europa.eu/system/files/2024-12/edpb_opinion_202428_ai-models_en.pdf
- **Guidelines on automated decision-making + profiling** — update 09.10.2025 — AI pre-screening bez human review ≈ zakazany (art. 22 RODO) — https://www.edpb.europa.eu/our-work-tools/our-documents/topic/automated-decision-profiling_en
- **Pseudonymisation 01/2025**, **Blockchain 02/2025**, **DSA/GDPR 03/2025**, **Scientific research 01/2026**
